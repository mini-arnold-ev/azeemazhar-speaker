Engagement Format

Fireside Chat

45–90 minutes. Intimate format for small groups. Works exceptionally well at board dinners, CEO roundtables, and private executive gatherings where real conversation matters more than performance.

Duration
45–90 min
Audience
8–30 people
Format
Moderated conversation
Setting
Private or semi-private

Why Fireside

The format where real questions get answered

In a keynote, Azeem delivers a prepared argument to a large room. In a fireside, the conversation goes where the room needs it to go.

For boards and senior executives grappling with AI strategy, this is usually the higher-value format. The questions are more specific, the discussion is frank, and the insights are directly applicable to your situation — not a generic audience's.

Azeem is a practising investor, board adviser, and technologist. He speaks the language of capital, governance, and strategy — not just AI research.

01

Pre-session call to understand your group's specific questions and AI maturity

02

A framing position from Azeem (15–20 min) to anchor the conversation

03

Moderated Q&A with your moderator or self-directed

04

Open discussion — questions go where the group needs them

05

Chatham House rules apply by default; everything stays in the room

Best Contexts

Board Dinner

The night before a major board decision. A moderated conversation with Azeem sets the intellectual context — what's real, what's hype, what peers are actually doing. 8–20 people.

↳ FTSE 100 boards, Fortune 500 chairs, PE portfolio company boards

CEO Roundtable

Peer CEOs across industries, convened to compare notes on AI strategy. Azeem moderates and contributes — equal parts facilitator and provocateur. 12–25 people.

↳ CEO networks, industry associations, leadership councils

Investor LP Gathering

For fund managers who want to offer LPs a substantive session on AI's impact on returns, not a pitch. An intimate conversation that earns trust through rigour. 15–40 people.

↳ PE firms, VC funds, sovereign wealth advisors

Executive Offsite

One session within a larger leadership programme. Azeem joins for 60–90 minutes to stress-test the leadership team's AI assumptions and sharpen their strategic framing. Up to 30 people.

↳ Fortune 500 leadership teams, consulting firm partner groups
