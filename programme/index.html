Programme Formats

Guest Faculty (2 Sessions)

Azeem joins an existing executive education programme as a guest expert. Two sessions — typically a keynote or briefing followed by an interactive workshop — provide participants with both the macro view and practical application.

↳ Business school exec ed, corporate university programmes

Learning outcomes: Shared framework for evaluating AI decisions · First-hand experience with frontier AI tools · Sector-specific competitive analysis

Half-Day AI Immersion

A concentrated programme combining briefing, live AI demonstration, and strategic working session. Teams leave with genuine first-hand intuition about AI capabilities — not a second-hand account.

↳ Board strategy days, leadership offsites, PE portfolio company days

Multi-Week Strategic Programme

Three to five sessions over weeks or months, building cumulative insight within your organisation. Each session advances the strategic conversation — from landscape orientation to sector-specific analysis to decision-making frameworks.

↳ Corporate transformation programmes, board education series, PE portfolio development

Learning outcomes: AI-literate leadership team · Prioritised action list for AI adoption · Governance framework for AI decisions · Sector-specific competitive positioning

How It Works

01

Scoping call

Initial conversation with your programme lead to understand learning objectives, audience profile, and how Azeem's sessions fit within the broader programme.

02

Co-design

Session structure and content tailored to your specific context. For exec ed programmes, this includes alignment with other faculty and case materials.

03

Delivery

Sessions delivered on your schedule. Each builds on the previous, creating a coherent arc from orientation to action.

04

Post-programme synthesis

A written synthesis of the strategic themes and questions that emerged — a document your leadership team can use as a foundation for next steps.
