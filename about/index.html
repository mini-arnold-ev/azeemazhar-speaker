About

Azeem Azhar

AI strategist · Author · Investor · Keynote speaker

Azeem Azhar has spent two decades at the intersection of technology, business, and society. He started as a journalist — reporting on the earliest days of the commercial internet — before becoming a technology entrepreneur, building and leading startups across media, AI, and e-commerce. He is an active investor in companies working on AI, energy, and other exponential technologies.

In 2015, he founded Exponential View, now one of the world's most influential technology analysis platforms with 286,000 subscribers and over 26 million articles read annually. His weekly newsletter and podcast are read and listened to by senior leaders in technology, finance, policy, and industry worldwide.

His book Exponential: How Accelerating Technology Is Leaving Us Behind and What to Do About It was named a Financial Times Best Book of 2021 and has been translated into 12 languages. It provides the economic and strategic framework that underpins his advisory and speaking work.

Azeem is an Executive Fellow at Harvard Business School, where he works on the economics of AI and exponential technologies. He co-chairs the World Economic Forum's Global Futures Council on the intersection of AI and economic strategy. He is a regular contributor to the BBC, Financial Times, Wired, and Bloomberg.

He speaks approximately 25 times per year — to boards, C-suites, investment committees, conference audiences, and executive education programmes. His work spans finance, technology, energy, healthcare, consumer goods, and professional services.
