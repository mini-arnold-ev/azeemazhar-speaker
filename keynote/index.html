Signature Topics

The AI Investment Thesis: Stampede or Bubble?

A rigorous look at AI valuations, capital flows, and the economics of foundation models. Where is real value being created — and where are investors paying for hype?

↳ Investor conferences, equity research events, LP meetings

Capital Deployment in the Exponential Age

Technology cost curves are collapsing exponentially — in compute, energy, biology, and manufacturing. What does this mean for portfolio construction, sector allocation, and the next decade of returns?

↳ PE partner days, asset manager offsites, sovereign wealth briefings

The $100 Trillion Productivity Question

AI promises transformative productivity gains — but the economic evidence is complex. This session separates the macro reality from the vendor pitch.

↳ CEO leadership meetings, board strategy sessions, industry conferences

What to Expect

Rigour over inspiration

Azeem's keynotes are built from primary research, not slides recycled from last year. Each engagement starts with a briefing call to understand your audience, their level of AI sophistication, and the specific questions they need answered.

The result is a session that moves a room — not because it's entertaining, but because it's true.
