Signature Topics

The AI Investment Thesis: Stampede or Bubble?

A rigorous look at AI valuations, capital flows, and the economics of foundation models. Where is real value being created — and where are investors paying for hype?

↳ Investor conferences, equity research events, LP meetings

How AI Changes Your Cost Structure

AI promises transformative productivity gains — but the evidence is complex. This session separates macro reality from vendor pitch, helping leaders understand where gains materialise and what it means for their business.

↳ CEO leadership meetings, board strategy sessions, industry conferences

The Exponential Age: What Business Leaders Need to Know

Technology cost curves in compute, energy, biology, and manufacturing are collapsing faster than institutions can adapt. The framework for understanding why — and what to do about it.

↳ Corporate leadership events, annual conferences, executive offsites

Energy, AI & the Great Rewrite

Three fundamental inputs to civilisation — energy, intelligence, and biology — have each crossed from extraction to engineering. Every institution built for scarcity is running yesterday's software.

↳ Energy conferences, sustainability summits, infrastructure investors

What to Expect

Rigour over inspiration

Azeem's keynotes are built from primary research, not slides recycled from last year. Each engagement starts with a briefing call to understand your audience, their level of AI sophistication, and the specific questions they need answered.

The result is a session that moves a room — not because it's entertaining, but because it's true.

45–60 minutes of substantive argument. No filler, no generic AI hype. A position, evidence, implications.
