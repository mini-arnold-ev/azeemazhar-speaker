The Brief

Keynote speaker & strategist on AI, exponential technologies, and the forces reshaping business.

Founder of Exponential View · Author of Exponential (FT Best Book 2021) · Executive Fellow, Harvard Business School

The Argument

A pragmatic optimist who has spent two decades building, investing in, and advising on exponential technologies.

AI and adjacent exponential technologies — energy, biotech, robotics — are collapsing cost curves at rates that make traditional forecasting unreliable. The organisations that understand this will move faster and more confidently. Those that don't will find their strategies built on yesterday's assumptions.

I don't just analyse exponential technologies — I invest in them, I've built companies with them, and I've spent a decade tracking the energy transition that powers them. When I brief a leadership team on AI economics, I'm drawing on operator experience, not just research.

I work with boards, C-suites, and conference audiences — from intimate sessions of eight to keynotes for thousands. Every engagement is tailored. Hybrid and virtual delivery available.
