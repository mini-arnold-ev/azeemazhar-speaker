Engagement Format

Workshop

2–3 hours. Combines AI briefing, live demonstration, and strategic working session. Teams leave with genuine first-hand intuition — not a second-hand account of what AI can do.

Duration
2–3 hours
Audience
8–50 people
Format
Presentation + live demo + working session
Output
Strategic framework + action list

Workshop Arc

01

Where We Are (30 min)

A rigorous briefing on the current state of AI — what's real, what's hype, where the economic value is actually forming. Calibrates the room to a shared factual baseline before strategy discussion.

02

Live AI Demonstration (45 min)

Hands-on use of frontier AI tools in real-time. Participants see models reason, write, analyse, and fail — building genuine intuition rather than a second-hand view of AI capabilities.

03

Strategic Implications for Your Organisation (45 min)

Working session applying the AI analysis to your specific business context. Where are your competitive risks? Which functions transform first? What decisions can't wait?

04

Framework & Next Steps (30 min)

Participants leave with a shared mental model for evaluating AI decisions and a prioritised short list of actions. Azeem facilitates, your team generates the outputs.

Why It Works

Seeing beats hearing

Most executive AI briefings are verbal — someone describes what AI can do. Azeem's workshops are different: participants interact with frontier models in real time, including seeing where they reason well and where they fail.

This firsthand experience changes the quality of the strategic conversation that follows. Teams stop debating whether AI is real and start discussing what to do about it.

Pre-workshop

Briefing call with your team lead. Azeem reviews your organisation's AI context, competitive landscape, and the specific decisions your leadership faces.

Day of

No slides until needed. Azeem opens with a tight briefing, moves immediately to live demonstration, then facilitates the working session.

Output

A shared strategic framework and a short prioritised action list — generated by your team, facilitated by Azeem.

Follow-on

Optional: Azeem reviews the action list 4–6 weeks later and provides a written perspective on progress.
